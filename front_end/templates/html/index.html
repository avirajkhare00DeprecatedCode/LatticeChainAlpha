About Lattice Protocol.

The sharing marketplace incepted with the idea of decentralization, has come to a standpoint of being a concentrated model, with a network strength in the grip of centralized organizations running them. The journey initially orchestrated by business models like Uber, Airbnb, Freelancer or We-Work is at a crossroad with a mojor part of the network benefit going to these organizations. The global economy now requires a perfectly distributed marketplace, which can leverage the effects of shared economy while through its architecture create a ground for value generation through network effect. This opens door for us to create the next mile in decentralization ecosystem, and give the world a state of the art indigenously designed Lattice protocol enabling a Smart digitally driven value economy.
